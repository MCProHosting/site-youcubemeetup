@YouCubeMeetup

With the recent post-poning of Mineorama a group of community members, YouTubers, and server hosts have came together with the intention of making the most of the situation.

YouCube Meetup is the result of a drive to rectify a wrongdoing in the community. On July 12th from 9AM to 6PM we'll be hosting a fan meetup with many who originally intended to attend Mineorama. No charge. No catch. Open to MineORama Attendees.

How to get in?

In order to get admitted to the Meetup you need to have your Mineorama ticket, which will not be collected. Registering is mandatory for admission.

Date: July 12th, 2014
Time: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM EST
Place: 125 West 18th Street New York, NY 10011

MineORama attendees, register here Follow For Updates

Anyone that is 15 or younger that is planning on attending has to be with a legal guardian.
